Our Services

Drawing upon our extensive knowledge and experience we manage, manufacture and deliver solutions time after time, our highly skilled team continue to achieve a high standard of quality and service. From maintenance and repair to providing new projects, everything we do is reliability centred, providing cost effective solutions tailored to our customers’ needs.

Fabrication

We offer a full Metal Inert Gas (MIG) fabrication service carried out by coded welders in mild steel, stainless steel as well as aluminium up to almost any thickness. Material can be supplied free issue, or we are able to source our own material from our trusted suppliers.

We can also offer subcontract laser cutting service produced from your own part drawings, ideal for prototyping or small batch work in a variety of finishes including paint, powder coating and hot dip galvanising.

Machining

Providing a bespoke machining service, we can supply precision made components, parts, prototypes and one off bespoke items in small to medium production runs, and can work most materials, including mild steel, copper, brass, aluminium, stainless steel, steel alloys, nylons and plastics. Operations include: turning, boring, drilling and tapping.
